Kolay Video Maker

Kolay is a powerful, AI-driven video editing app that enables users to create professional-quality short videos and vlogs easily on their mobile devices. With over 8,000 templates, 1,000+ music tracks, and cutting-edge features like digital human effects and AIGC-powered animations, Kolay simplifies video creation for users of all skill levels.

Template-Based Editing: Users can choose from thousands of customizable templates to create videos for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and other platforms.

AI-Powered Tools: The app offers intelligent background removal, digital human interactions, and auto-generated animations to enhance creativity.

Music & Effects: Kolay includes a rich music library and cinematic effects to bring videos to life.

Export Without Watermarks: Users can export videos in various resolutions, optimized for social media platforms, without watermarks.

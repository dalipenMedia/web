Privacy Policy for Kolay


Effective Date: December 17, 2024


At Dalipen Media LLC, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use our Kolay mobile application (“Kolay”). By using Kolay, you consent to the collection and processing of your information as outlined in this policy.


1. Information Collection

We collect both personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information to improve our services and your user experience.

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you use Kolay:

  • Your name, email address, and other contact information provided by you voluntarily.

  • Media files from your photo library, which you upload to create videos.

  • Camera access for capturing content.

1.2 Usage Data

We collect non-personally identifiable data related to your use of Kolay:

  • Device type, operating system, and IP address.

  • Information about the features of the app you access and how you interact with the app.

  • Network activity related to accessing remote AIGC services.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve Kolay’s services.

  • To create, customize, and share videos using your media files.

  • To interact with third-party services for video creation, including AIGC services.

  • To send notifications, updates, and marketing communications.

3. Data Sharing

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in delivering Kolay’s services, such as hosting, data analysis, and video processing. These providers are obligated to protect your information.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal data if required by law or to comply with legal processes.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to secure your data. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of your information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Kolay is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will take effect as soon as we post the updated policy in the app or on our website. Please review the policy regularly.

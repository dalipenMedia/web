About Us

Discover our story and innovative video production tools

Our Story

We are a startup company born in Colorado, USA. We specialize in making video production tools that run on mobile phones. Using our unique technology, you can perform multiple editing functions on your phone.

Our Features

Video Editing: Editing tools allow users to trim, cut, merge and adjust videos to create seamless and engaging videos.

Effects and Filters: Various effects, filters, and animated stickers that users can add to their videos to enhance their visual appeal.

Text and typography: You can add text to your video by choosing from a range of fonts, colors, and styles to create captions, titles, or overlay text.

Music and Sound Effects: You can add background music from the audio track library to your video. Additionally, users can add sound effects to enhance the overall video experience.

Templates and Themes: You get free templates that users can apply to their videos and create short videos or status videos for your social media. These templates include creative transitions, animations, and layouts to simplify video creation.

Collage and Split Screen: You can create a collage-style video or a split-screen video by merging multiple clips into a single frame.

Reverse and slow motion effects: You can reverse video playback or apply slow motion effects.

Social Sharing: After editing and exporting your videos, you can easily share them to various social media platforms like YouTube, Instagram, etc. directly from the app.

DIY Video Creation: It may offer a "DIY" mode where you can create videos using a step-by-step process, choosing from available templates, effects, and other elements.

Photo Slideshow: You can create photo slideshows with transitions, music, and effects.

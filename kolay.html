Kolay Video Maker

Introduce

Kolay is a video editing application that provides you with various features to create amazing videos, and you can use it to accomplish almost all video effects.

Features

  • 10000+ Template
  • 500+ Effect
  • 500+ Stickers
  • Fully customizable text effects

How To Use

Here are the steps on how to use the Kolay app:

  1. Open the Kolay app on your phone
  2. Choose a template or create your own video.
  3. Choose your phone and add music, effects, stickers and text to your video.
  4. You can edit videos by trimming, cropping and adjusting.
  5. Finally, export the video and share it on social media such as YouTube, Instagram, Moj or others.
